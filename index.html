About Me

Hi! I'm Ansh Kuckreja

I'm a 16 year-old student from Toronto. Living in one of the most up-and-coming tech cities in the world, I am always surrounded by revolutionary technologies and industries. Recently, I've been keeping up with AI, cryptourrencies, and cellular agriculture.

In my opinion, global exposure and being given first-hand experience is imperative to personal growth. A week-long service trip taught me so much that it has led be to believe that first-hand exposure to different cultures and lifestyles should be much more popularized.
